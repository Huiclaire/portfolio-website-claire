About Me

My Intro

hi there - I am a Germany-based junior full-stack developer from Taiwan.

After 4 years of work experience in the art industry, I transitioned into the crypto industry, which led me to join a full-stack web development bootcamp and move to Germany.

This career change decision is driven by my desire to become an active part of using technology to help create value for the world we live in.

In my professional life, I have gained in-depth experience in content marketing and I thoroughly enjoyed presenting projects in a way that inspires, engages, and drives action. Having worked in high-stress, cross-functional and cross-culture environments has also made me a strong team-player, who is empathetic, collaborative, and open to feedback.

In my free time, I enjoy traveling, cycling in the mountain, surfing in the ocean, and cuddling with my cats.
